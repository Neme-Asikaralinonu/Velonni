About Velonni

Crafting Fashion Beyond Seasons.
Celebrating Authentic Confidence.

Our Philosophy

Velonni is not just a fashion house; it’s a movement. We believe fashion is a language—a personal statement that should empower, uplift, and inspire.

Every piece we create is rooted in the idea of timeless beauty, bold confidence, and cultural authenticity. We merge tradition with innovation, crafting garments that make you feel like the best version of yourself.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to break the mold of mainstream fashion, Velonni began as a boutique atelier in Lagos, Nigeria. Our founders—visionaries in both design and craftsmanship—sought to build a brand that blends high fashion with cultural pride.

From handcrafted gowns to avant-garde menswear, we’ve expanded into a global name without losing our roots. Each collection honors African heritage, modern innovation, and luxurious detailing.

The Craft

At Velonni, craftsmanship is sacred. We use premium fabrics sourced responsibly, and every stitch is placed with precision.

Our artisans spend hours hand-finishing garments to ensure each piece meets the highest standards of luxury. From initial sketches to final fittings, we blend tradition with technology to create wearable art.

Why Choose Velonni?

  • Authentic Luxury: We prioritize quality over mass production.
  • Bold Individuality: Our collections are made for those who dare to stand out.
  • Ethical Fashion: Sustainability is part of our DNA.
  • Tailored Experiences: From personal styling to private fittings, every service is bespoke.
